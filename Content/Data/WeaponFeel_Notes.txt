- Always feels usable.

- Primary role: Kaiju DPS and weak point chip.

Rocket Launcher Intent
- "Comedy chaos" tool.
- Slower cadence, huge payoff.
- Environmental bias: encourages collateral + crowd-clearing.
- Primary role: stagger setup moments and cinematic knockback.
