Primary Design Goal

Display Rules
- Timer: large, always visible.
- Destruction meter: bar + percent text.
- Kaiju health: bar + percent.
- Weak points: 3 compact bars (color shift when destroyed).
- Stagger indicator: large short-lived banner.
- Scoreboard: Top-N list; always include local players even if not top.

Layout Guidance
- Keep HUD within safe zones for split-screen.
- Avoid small labels; prefer icons + large numerics.
- Use short text strings (e.g., "KAIJU", "CITY", "TIME").
